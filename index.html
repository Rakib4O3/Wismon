Privacy-First Philosophy

We believe your personal data should stay personal. Here's how we protect your privacy.

✓ No Data Collection • Privacy Guaranteed
📱

Local-First Storage

All your saved quotes, preferences, and app data stays exclusively on your device. No cloud storage, no external servers.

🌐

Smart Quote Fetching

Fresh inspirational content from curated online sources, fetched anonymously without tracking your activity.

🔒

Zero Personal Data

We don't collect, store, or process any personal information. No accounts, no profiles, no tracking.

📊
Data We Don't Collect

Wismon operates with complete privacy respect. We don't collect personal information, usage analytics, device identifiers, location data, or any other tracking data. Your interaction with the app remains completely private.

💾
Local Data Storage

Your saved quotes, app preferences, daily statistics, and notification settings are stored locally on your device using Android's secure SharedPreferences system. This data never leaves your device.

🌍
Online Content Access

When enabled, the app fetches inspirational quotes from curated online sources to provide fresh content. These requests are made anonymously without sending any personal data or device information.

🔔
Notifications & Background Tasks

Daily inspirational notifications are generated locally on your device using Android's WorkManager. No data is sent to external servers for notification processing.

Your Complete Control

Disable online quotes, turn off notifications, or clear all saved data anytime through the app settings. You have full control over your Wismon experience and data.

🔗
Third-Party Services

Wismon may access external quote sources and uses Android system services for sharing and notifications. No personal data is shared with these services, and all interactions remain anonymous.

📱
App Permissions

The app requests only essential permissions: Internet access for fetching quotes, notification permissions for daily inspiration, and wake lock for reliable background notifications. All permissions are used transparently and only for their stated purpose.

Questions About Privacy?

We're committed to transparency. Reach out if you have any privacy concerns.
